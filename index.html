About me

Hi, I’m Diwash Gautam, a biotechnology undergraduate passionate about exploring how science can enhance health, food systems, and everyday living. My interests include molecular nutrition, the microbiome, bioprocess simulation and engineering, population genetics, behavioral biology and social psychology.

I’m particularly drawn to the FMCG and hospitality industries and to post-harvest loss minimization, where I aim to apply evidence-based, science-driven approaches to create sustainable and innovative solutions.

I also run Wellora with Jeevan, a blog where I share research-backed insights on nutrition, lifestyle, genetics, and medicine under the themes Discover, Balance, and Thrive. Looking ahead, I aspire to build a career that bridges wellness, research, consulting, and product innovation all grounded in scientific evidence and real-world applications.
